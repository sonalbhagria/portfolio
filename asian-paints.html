3D Room Visualiser

Tablet · Android

A 3D tablet experience that lets users walk through a virtual room and apply Asian Paints' Wallfashion textures and finishes to any surface — seeing the result in context before committing to a choice. Guided onboarding ensured first-time users could explore independently without needing a sales advisor.

Exterior Visualiser
