Overview

The challenge

Streaming products live or die on experience. A single friction point — a slow browse, a confusing remote interaction, a broken playback state — is enough to lose a subscriber. The challenge was to design a coherent, high-quality experience across five fundamentally different platforms, each with its own interaction paradigm, input method, and screen context.

Working within Incedo's cross-functional team structure, and in close collaboration with US-based product stakeholders, I led UX design across the full platform suite — from small phone screens to the 10-foot TV experience.

My role

What I led

As Technical Lead for UX, I owned the design process across all platforms — conducting research, developing wireframes and prototypes, and maintaining design quality through implementation. I managed multiple client projects simultaneously while coordinating with engineering and product teams across time zones.

A core part of the work was maintaining a coherent design vision across platforms without forcing a single UI pattern onto fundamentally different contexts.

"A great streaming experience is invisible. The moment a viewer notices the UI, you've already lost them."

Platforms

Five surfaces, one experience

iOS

Touch-first. Gesture navigation and thumb-zone optimisation.

Android

Material patterns with custom visual identity layered on top.

Apple TV

Focus-based navigation. Siri Remote gestural interactions.

Fire TV

D-pad navigation with Alexa voice integration.

Xbox

Controller navigation. High-contrast 10-foot UI patterns.

Process

Research & design methods

01

Platform research

Deep-dived into each platform's HIG and design conventions — understanding where to follow platform norms and where to diverge for brand consistency.

02

User research

Conducted persona creation, usability testing, and questionnaire studies — adapting methods to cover both casual mobile viewers and lean-back TV audiences.

03

Wireframing & prototyping

Developed user flows, wireframes, and interactive prototypes for each platform — iterating rapidly and validating key interaction models before visual design.

04

Cross-team delivery

Collaborated with US-based product teams to align on vision, then worked closely with engineering to maintain design fidelity through delivery.

Outcomes

Key results

5

Platforms shipped

Delivered cohesive UX across iOS, Android, Apple TV, Fire TV, and Xbox — each optimised for its native interaction paradigm.

↑

Interaction quality

Introduced structured UX process to a team that previously lacked it — improving product quality and reducing design-engineering churn.

✦

Cross-platform coherence
