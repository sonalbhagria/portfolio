About me

The designer behind the work

I believe the best design is invisible — it removes friction, surfaces insight, and makes complexity feel effortless.

With a background spanning B2B platforms, media intelligence, and multi-platform consumer products, I bring a research-first, systems-minded approach to product design. At the EIU I lead end-to-end UX for global insight platforms, partnering closely with product and engineering to translate complex requirements into scalable experiences.

I'm actively exploring the frontier of AI-assisted UX — from synthetic research methods to responsible AI design practices — and sharing that work through speaking engagements.
